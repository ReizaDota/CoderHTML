Our History

The East India Company was created in 1602 as an almagamation of existing smaller companies and enterprises. It's main goal has been to trade spices from Asia to Europe among other notable goods. The company experienced a heavy growth throughout its expansion period during the XVII to XIX centuries, creating a trading Empire over South East Asia and Europe. It manage to establish a monopoly over the region which was virtually uncontested until the XX century. Entering this new era, the East India Company underwent severe transformations to evolve into this new everchanging world and, most notably, to continue being the number one choiceto satisfy your every need of spices.
